I'm Olaya Álvarez-Tuñón, a Postdoctoral Researcher with a passion for Robotics, Computer Vision, and Deep Learning. I completed my Ph.D. in Robotics and Computer Vision at [Aarhus University](https://international.au.dk/), and I'm currently working at [EIVA A/S](https://www.eiva.com/) while collaborating with the Software Quality Research (SQUARE) group at the [IT University of Copenhagen (ITU)](https://en.itu.dk/).

My research focuses on Visual Simultaneous Localization and Mapping (SLAM), Deep Learning, and Computer Vision — with an emphasis on visual-based navigation for underwater and safety-critical applications.

As a roboticist, I have hands-on experience across hardware and software: designing and maintaining robotic systems, and implementing state-of-the-art algorithms on robots using ROS (1 and 2) and libraries such as behavior trees. I also care deeply about science dissemination and clearly documented software — see my [blog](/blog/) and [publications](/publications/).
