I am one of the organizers of the **[AI for Marine Perception](https://www.aicentre.dk/p1-programs/ai-for-marine-perception)** P1 program at the [Pioneer Centre for Artificial Intelligence](https://www.aicentre.dk/) (Denmark's national AI research centre, hosted by DTU, AU, KU, ITU, and Aalborg University).

The program catalyzes interdisciplinary collaboration between researchers and practitioners working at the intersection of **AI, marine science, and underwater robotics**. Marine environments pose unique challenges — limited light, complex sound propagation, and highly dynamic conditions — that prevent the straightforward application of conventional AI. We identify shared research challenges, build community, and run workshops on topics such as multimodal learning, marine robotics autonomy, and environmental monitoring.
