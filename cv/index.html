Professional Summary

Computer Vision and Deep Learning Engineer with extensive expertise in visual odometry and Visual SLAM. My research focuses on machine learning and computer vision within robotics, designing optimal architectures grounded in a geometric understanding of deep learning models. As a roboticist I have hands-on experience with both hardware and software, using ROS (1 and 2) and libraries such as behavior trees.
