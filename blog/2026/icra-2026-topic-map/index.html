ICRA 2026: an interactive topic map

Created on June 10, 2026

2026   ·   robotics   ICRA   visualization   ·   robotics

Every ICRA paper this year — all 3,028 of them, proceedings and workshop papers together — clustered into 43 topics and wired into one graph. Each node is a topic, sized by how many papers it holds; an edge means two topics share papers, so the layout pulls related areas together (SLAM next to localization, manipulation next to imitation learning, and so on).

How to use it: type in the search box to match papers by title or author — the graph lights up the topics those papers fall in and the panel lists every match. Click a highlighted topic to refine the list to that topic. With the box empty, click any topic to browse its papers, and click a paper title to read its abstract.
